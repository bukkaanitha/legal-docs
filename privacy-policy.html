Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Numly ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to the Numly mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Numly does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.

The App is designed to work completely offline and does not require:

  • Account registration
  • Personal information
  • Login credentials
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers

Data Storage

All data created within the App is stored locally on your device only:

  • Calculation History: Saved calculations are stored locally using your device's storage (AsyncStorage)
  • App Settings: Any preferences are stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not sync, backup, or transfer your data to any servers

You can clear this local data at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear All" button in each calculator
  • Uninstalling the app from your device

Permissions

The App requests the following permission:

VIBRATE

  • Purpose: To provide haptic feedback when you press calculator buttons
  • Usage: Only activated when you interact with the app
  • No Data Collection: This permission does not allow us to collect any information

Third-Party Services

Numly does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

We do not use:

  • Google Analytics
  • Facebook SDK
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Any other third-party tracking services

Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is suitable for users of all ages and contains no inappropriate content.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any data:

  • There is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our App
  • All calculations remain private on your device
  • No data breaches can occur as there is no data transmitted to external servers

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data:

  • There is no data to access, modify, or delete from our servers
  • You have complete control over any locally stored data on your device
  • You can uninstall the app at any time to remove all local data

Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • Google Play Store policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
